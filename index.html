Frequently Asked Questions

Is this SAHPRA and HPCSA compliant?

Yes! The tracker includes all required fields per Medicines and Related Substances Act, General Regulations (GN R510), and Good Pharmacy Practice guidelines. It's used by 100+ practices and has passed multiple inspector visits.

Do I need technical skills to use this?

No! If you can use basic Excel or Google Sheets, you can use this. We provide 140+ pages of step-by-step documentation with screenshots. Setup takes under 1 hour following our guide.

Does this work on Mac and Windows?

Yes! The tracker is Excel-based (works on Windows/Mac) and optimized for Google Sheets (works anywhere with internet). Use whichever you prefer.

What about barcode scanners? Do I need to buy one?

No, barcode scanning is optional but recommended. The tracker works perfectly without a scanner (manual entry). If you want one, we recommend the Zebra DS2208 (R1,500) which plugs into USB and works instantly.

Can I import my existing stock data?

Yes! If you have stock data in Excel/CSV format, we can help you import it. Professional and Enterprise packages include data migration assistance.

How do updates work?

Essential package gets free updates for 1 year. Professional and Enterprise get lifetime updates. When regulations change, we update the tracker and notify all customers.

What if I'm not satisfied?

30-day money-back guarantee, no questions asked. If the tracker doesn't work for your practice, we'll refund you in full.

Do you offer training for my staff?

Yes! Professional package includes 20-minute training video. Enterprise package includes 4-hour on-site training. Additional training workshops available for R1,200.
